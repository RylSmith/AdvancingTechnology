What is Nanotechnology?

Nanotechnology studies unique property changes on the nanoscale — between one and 100 nanometers, which is one-billionth of a meter — by way of manipulating atoms and molecules.

Nanotechnology is all around us. Below you can find one great example of how Nanotechnology is used in everyday life.

Computers are a great example of Nanotechnology in action because so many parts of the computer use Nano Tech. For example, Nano tech is used in computer chips, quantum computing, and robotics.
