What is 3D Printing?

3D printing is a process in which a digital model is turned into a tangible, solid, three-dimensional object, usually by laying down many successive, thin layers of a material. 3D printing has become popular so quickly because it makes manufacturing accessible to more people than ever before.

What could 3D Printing be used for in the future?

3D printing has many different uses and as it continues to improve it will continue to have more uses. One big example of how it may be used is in construction with being able to print housing even if that concept is far in the future.
